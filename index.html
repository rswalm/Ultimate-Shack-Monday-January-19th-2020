Meet Our CEO Larry Swalm

Sales/Project Development role allows him to educate clients on the design-build group’s services and then comprehensively develop each project. He walks clients through the preliminary design process with 3D designs, effectively defines the scope of the project, coordinates the development of construction drawings and assists every client with the permitting process. With more than 34 years of experience in 3D design and a longstanding history and knowledge of construction, he provides unmatched support to Gilbert + Burke’s clientele. His past work includes more than two decades in engineering and design in the packaging industry with a global customer base, 11-plus years in sales and design in the custom package/prefab home industry and more than 25 years of owning and operating his own custom design firm. Larry has a degree in industrial design and won a scholarship for outstanding achievement during his education. While he was in school, he worked for several custom home builders to develop a deeper understanding of construction and to hone his passion for architectural design. Larry has a long-standing passion for energy-efficient design and construction, including Net-Zero and Passive House™. He even designed and constructed his own Hybrid Timber-Frame home, where he currently lives and continues to perform his own home renovations. He has held the roles of Secretary and Board Chairman for a local nonprofit organization, and in his spare time, he enjoys playing and watching basketball.

  • The Design Process

    Starts With You

    .

    STEP 1: Call us. Itʻs free. Weʻll help you understand the process and offer a free estimate of our services. STEP 2: Actually the first step in the design process; its all about getting to know you. We want to know what makes you tick, the things you love, the things you hate, the things you canʻt live without, the best and worst experiences youʻve had with current or previous living spaces. This includes helping you to convey to us a firm understanding of your budget. Weʻll also walk your building lot with you and help you understand all potential limitations. STEP 3: We start every project by designing with 3D shaded modelling and YOU are our partner in this very important process; YOU are the 4th dimension. We are now partners and allies in your design/build project. We treat each design decision as if it was our house we are designing and our money being spent. We design in 3D and provide opportunities throughout the process to make changes dynamically with you. Imagine sitting together at your kitchen table and reviewing your home in 3D with all furnishings visible. By the time we proceed to the detailed construction drawing phase there will not be one cubic inch of space in your home that you don’t understand and love. No “guessing games” means no disappointments and a lot less stress throughout every stage of the process. Our goal as your ally is to help design your project within your budget and help make the entire process as stress free as possible. STEP 4: With a well thought out 3-D preliminary plan we are ready to head into the Construction Drawing phase. Our BCIN approved construction drawings will include all necessary information as required by your local building department. We will also include specific details that your contractor might wish to see on the drawings. We have a team of engineers & associates to help with Structural Engineering, HVAC, Site Grading, Surveying and more. STEP 5: As your build project begins and develops we are always available to answer questions or provide supplemental information until the project is complete.
